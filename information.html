Making sense of the data

What does this system do?

The system continually tracks five metrics:

PM2.5, PM5, PM10, TVOC, ECo2

These five metrics are compressed into a single dimensional index that forms that basis for this data visualization.

How does it work?

Build atop a Raspberry Pi, the system uses several connected hardware sensors that continuously cycle air from the ITP/IMA fabrication shop. The system is designed to measure the ambient air of the roomrather the quality of the air coming out of any one machine.
